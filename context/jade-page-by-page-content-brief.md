# Page-by-Page Content Brief — Jade Alombro Portfolio Website

## Global messaging direction

### Brand line
**Jade Alombro — WordPress Developer**

### Core description
Jade is a **web developer specializing in WordPress** who builds custom business websites and WooCommerce solutions with a focus on reliability, maintainability, and real business needs.

### Tone
- approachable and business-friendly
- technical and credible
- premium and minimal

### Core trust outcomes
Visitors should feel:
- Jade knows what she’s doing
- Jade understands business needs, not just design
- Jade can be trusted with an important project
